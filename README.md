# 🌿 Jeevdhara: AI-Powered Biodiversity & Reforestation

**Jeevdhara** is a comprehensive ecosystem platform designed to protect life on land. By combining advanced AI vision with community-driven action, we bridge the gap between NGOs, Landowners, Volunteers, and Corporate Partners to accelerate reforestation and biodiversity monitoring.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔍 AI Biodiversity Scanner
Leveraging **Gemini 2.5 Flash**, users can capture or upload images of local flora and fauna. The AI identifies the species, assesses its threat level, legal status, and provides an immediate ecological action plan.

### 🗺️ Community Feed
- **Plantation Drives:** NGOs and Companies can organize and fund reforestation events.
- **Land Offering:** Landowners can offer degraded land for restoration projects.
- **GPS-Based Sorting:** Volunteers can find the closest initiatives to their current location.

### 🏆 Gamification & Chief Ranger Message
To encourage consistent conservation, users earn points and badges for every unique species scanned. A dynamic "Chief Ranger" message (powered by GPT-OSS) provides personalized motivation based on leaderboard performance.

### 🏢 Multi-Role Ecosystem
Custom dashboards and onboarding flows for:
- **Volunteers:** Join drives and earn rewards.
- **NGOs:** Manage volunteers and request resources.
- **Landowners:** Monitor reforestation on their property.
- **Companies:** Track CSR and Carbon Offset impact.
